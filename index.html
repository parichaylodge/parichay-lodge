Our Story

A Lodge Built on
Warmth & Welcome

Parichay Lodge was born from a simple belief — every traveller deserves a clean, comfortable, and genuinely welcoming place to rest, even for just a night.

Nestled in Hassan — the gateway to the Hoysala heritage sites of Belur and Halebidu — we serve pilgrims, tourists, and business travellers with equal care and attention. Our staff are locals who know this land deeply.

What's Nearby

Hassan is the perfect base to explore some of Karnataka's most iconic destinations.

Belur Temple

38 km — The crown jewel of Hoysala architecture, featuring exquisite 12th-century stone carvings.

~45 min drive

Halebidu

32 km — Twin temple complex with some of the most intricate stone sculptures in all of India.

~40 min drive

Gorur Dam

16 km — A serene backwater landscape on the Hemavathi River, perfect for evening outings.

~20 min drive

Hasanamba Temple

2 km — Ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Hasanamba, open only a few days a year — a rare spiritual experience.

~5 min drive

Sravanabelagola

49 km — Home to the towering 18-metre monolithic statue of Gomateshwara, one of the tallest Jain shrines in the world.

~1 hr drive

Settihalli Church

40 km — A hauntingly beautiful submerged church that emerges from the Hemavathi reservoir during summer — a photographer's dream.

~50 min drive

Sakleshpur

58 km — Misty Western Ghats hill station with lush coffee estates and trekking trails.

~1.5 hr drive

Chikkamagaluru

77 km — The coffee capital of India, nestled in the Western Ghats with scenic hills, waterfalls and trekking routes.

~1.5 hr drive
